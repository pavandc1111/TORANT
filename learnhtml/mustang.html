The Ford Mustang Sport is an iconic American muscle car, blending classic design with modern performance and technology.

Engine: 5.0L Ti-VCT V8 or 2.3L EcoBoost Turbocharged Inline-4.
Power: 450–480 bhp / 556 Nm torque (V8).
Transmission: 6-speed Manual / 10-speed Automatic with paddle shifters.
Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 4.2 seconds (V8).
Top Speed: 250 km/h (electronically limited). BOOK

The Ford Bronco is a rugged off-road SUV designed for adventure, combining classic styling with modern capability and technology.

Engine: 2.3L EcoBoost Turbo Inline-4 / 2.7L Twin-Turbo V6.
Power: 275–330 bhp / 440–563 Nm torque.
Transmission: 7-speed Manual / 10-speed Automatic.
Drivetrain: 4x4 with Terrain Management System (G.O.A.T. Modes).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 6.7 seconds (V6).
Top Speed: Around 200 km/h. BOOK

The Ford Escape is a compact SUV that offers a perfect blend of comfort, efficiency, and advanced technology for urban and family driving.

Engine: 1.5L EcoBoost Turbo Inline-3 / 2.0L EcoBoost Turbo Inline-4 / 2.5L Hybrid.
Power: 180–250 bhp / 250–380 Nm torque.
Transmission: 8-speed Automatic (Torque Converter) / eCVT for Hybrid models.
Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) / optional All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 6.9 seconds (2.0L model).
Top Speed: Around 200 km/h. BOOK

The Ford Endeavour is a premium full-size SUV known for its bold design, powerful performance, and exceptional off-road capability.

Engine: 2.0L EcoBlue Turbo Diesel / 3.2L Duratorq TDCi Diesel (older variant).
Power: 170–200 bhp / 420–500 Nm torque.
Transmission: 10-speed Automatic / 6-speed Automatic (earlier models).
Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD) / optional 4x4 with Terrain Management System.
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 10.6 seconds (2.0L).
Top Speed: Approximately 180 km/h. BOOK
