The Range Rover Evoque is a stylish compact luxury SUV known for its premium design, comfort, and refined performance.

Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Petrol / 2.0L Ingenium Diesel.
Power: 177–246 bhp / 365–430 Nm torque.
Transmission: 9-speed Automatic.
Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD) with Terrain Response.
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 7.5–9.0 seconds (depending on engine).
Top Speed: Approximately 220 km/h. BOOK

The Range Rover Sport is a premium performance SUV offering a blend of luxury, power, and dynamic handling with advanced off-road capability.

Engine: 3.0L Inline-6 Petrol / 3.0L Diesel / 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (depending on variant).
Power: 355–523 bhp / up to 750 Nm torque.
Transmission: 8-speed Automatic (Torque Converter).
Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD) with Terrain Response 2.
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 4.5–6.0 seconds (depending on engine).
Top Speed: Around 234–250 km/h. BOOK

The Jaguar F-PACE is a luxury performance SUV that blends sporty driving dynamics with premium comfort and modern technology.

Power: 247–542 bhp / up to 700 Nm torque.
Transmission: 8-speed Automatic.
Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 4.0–7.3 seconds (depending on variant).
