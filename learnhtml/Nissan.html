The Nissan GT-R, often nicknamed “Godzilla,” is a high-performance sports car that blends cutting-edge technology with breathtaking power and precision handling.

Engine: 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (VR38DETT).
Power: 565–600 bhp / 633–652 Nm torque.
Transmission: 6-speed Dual-Clutch Automatic with paddle shifters.
Drivetrain: Advanced ATTESA E-TS All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 2.9 seconds.
Top Speed: 315 km/h. BOOK

The Nissan X-Trail is a stylish and versatile family SUV that combines comfort, advanced safety, and efficient performance for both city and adventure driving.

Engine: 1.5L VC-Turbo 3-cylinder with e-POWER Hybrid System.
Power: 201–211 bhp (combined output) / 330–360 Nm torque.
Transmission: Xtronic CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission).
Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) / optional e-4ORCE All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 8.0 seconds.
Top Speed: Approximately 190 km/h. BOOK

The Nissan Qashqai (2021) is a stylish compact SUV that combines efficiency, comfort, and modern technology.

Engine: 1.3L Turbo Petrol with Mild-Hybrid system.
Power: 138–156 bhp / 240–270 Nm torque.
Transmission: 6-speed Manual / Xtronic CVT Automatic.
Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) / optional All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 9.5 seconds.
Top Speed: Approximately 200 km/h. BOOK

The Nissan Titan is a full-size pickup truck built primarily for the North American market.

Engine: 5.6L "Endurance" V8 gasoline.
Power: 400 hp / 560 Nm torque.
Transmission: 9-speed Automatic.
Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD) / optional Four-Wheel Drive (4WD).
Acceleration & Hauling: Towing capacity around 9,000 lbs (~4,082 kg) depending on configuration.
Top Speed: Not broadly published for all versions. BOOK
