The Toyota Fortuner is a rugged and premium full-size SUV combining strong off-road capability with modern family-SUV luxury.

Engine: 2.8 L Turbo-Diesel (also 2.7 L Petrol in some markets).
Power: ~201 bhp / ~500 Nm torque (diesel). :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The Toyota Vellfire is a premium luxury MPV known for its comfort, elegance, and advanced hybrid technology, offering a first-class travel experience.

Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder Petrol-Hybrid (E-Four system).
Power: 190–197 bhp (combined output) / 240 Nm torque.
Transmission: e-CVT (Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission).
Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD) with Hybrid Electric Assist.
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 8.1 seconds.
Top Speed: Approximately 170 km/h. BOOK

The Toyota Camry is a premium executive sedan that combines refined styling, hybrid efficiency, and smooth performance.

Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder Petrol-Hybrid.
Power: 218 bhp (combined output) / 221 Nm torque.
Transmission: e-CVT (Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission).
Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 8.5 seconds.
Top Speed: Approximately 180 km/h. BOOK

The Toyota Land Cruiser 70 is a tough, old-school SUV built for durability, reliability, and hardcore off-road performance at a budget-friendly price.

Engine: 2.8L Turbo-Diesel (new update) / 4.5L V8 Diesel (older markets).
Power: 201 bhp / 500 Nm torque (2.8L).
Transmission: 6-speed Automatic / 5-speed Manual (depending on market).
Drivetrain: Full-Time 4-Wheel Drive (4WD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 11 seconds (2.8L).
Top Speed: Approximately 170 km/h. BOOK
