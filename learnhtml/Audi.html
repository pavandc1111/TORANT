The Audi A4 is a premium luxury sedan known for its refined design, advanced technology, and smooth turbocharged performance.

Engine: 2.0L TFSI Turbocharged Petrol (mild-hybrid).
Power: 187–241 bhp / 320–370 Nm torque.
Transmission: 7-speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic.
Drivetrain: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) / optional Quattro All-Wheel Drive.
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 6.3–7.3 seconds (depending on variant).
Top Speed: Electronically limited to 250 km/h. BOOK

The Audi Q5 is a premium mid-size luxury SUV known for its refined performance, comfort, and advanced Quattro all-wheel-drive capability.

Engine: 2.0L TFSI Turbocharged Petrol (mild-hybrid).
Power: 249 bhp / 370 Nm torque.
Transmission: 7-speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic.
Drivetrain: Quattro All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 6.3 seconds.
Top Speed: Approximately 237 km/h. BOOK

The Audi Q7 is a premium full-size luxury SUV known for its powerful performance, spacious cabin, and advanced Quattro AWD system.

Engine: 3.0L TFSI V6 Turbocharged Petrol (mild-hybrid).
Power: 340 bhp / 500 Nm torque.
Transmission: 8-speed Tiptronic Automatic.
Drivetrain: Quattro All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in around 5.9 seconds.
Top Speed: Approximately 250 km/h (electronically limited). BOOK

The 2022 Audi RS6 Avant is a high-performance super wagon that blends brutal power with practicality and luxury.

Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 with 48V Mild-Hybrid.
Power: 591 bhp / 800 Nm torque.
Transmission: 8-speed Tiptronic Automatic.
Drivetrain: Quattro All-Wheel Drive (AWD).
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds.
Top Speed: 250 km/h (305 km/h with Dynamic Package). BOOK
